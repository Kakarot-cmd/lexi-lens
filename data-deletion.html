LexiLore: AR Vocabulary Quest — Data Deletion Request

Last updated: April 29, 2026

How to delete your account and data

Option 1 — From inside the app (recommended):
Open LexiLore → Log in → Tap the 📊 Word Tome button → Enter your Parent PIN → Scroll to bottom → Tap "Request Account Deletion"
Option 2 — By email:
Send a deletion request to beautifulhearts.n@gmail.com with subject line "Data Deletion Request" and your registered email address.

What gets deleted

Upon confirmed deletion: parent account, all child profiles, all quest progress, all scanned word history, and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

What is retained

Anonymous, non-identifiable crash diagnostic data (Sentry) may be retained for up to 90 days for app stability purposes.
