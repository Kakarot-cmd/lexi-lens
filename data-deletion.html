Lexi-Lens — Data Deletion Request

Last updated: May 7, 2026

How to delete your account and data

Option 1 — From inside the app (recommended):
Open Lexi-Lens → Sign in as the parent → tap the 📊 Word Tome button → enter your Parent PIN → scroll to the bottom → tap "Request Account Deletion" → confirm.
Option 2 — By email:
Send a message to beautifulhearts.n@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and the email address you registered with. We respond within 5 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days of confirmation.

What gets deleted

Upon confirmed deletion, the following are permanently removed within 30 days:

  • Your parent account and authentication record
  • Every child profile linked to the account
  • All quest progress, achievements, streaks, and unlocked spells
  • All scanned word history (the Word Tome)
  • All scan attempts and verdicts
  • Any verdict reports your household has filed
  • All consent records (retained only in anonymised audit form for the legal minimum)

What is retained

Anonymous, non-identifiable crash diagnostic data (Sentry) may be retained for up to 90 days for app stability purposes. This data is not linked to your account and cannot be used to re-identify you after deletion. Aggregated, fully de-identified usage statistics may be retained indefinitely for product analytics.

Cancelling a deletion request

After requesting deletion, you have a 30-day grace period during which you can cancel the request by signing back in — the app will show a banner offering to restore your data. Once the 30 days elapse, the data is permanently gone and cannot be recovered.

Contact

For any question about this process, contact beautifulhearts.n@gmail.com.
