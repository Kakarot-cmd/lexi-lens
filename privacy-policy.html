LexiLore: AR Vocabulary Quest — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Camera

The camera is used solely for real-time object recognition to support vocabulary learning. No images or video are stored, transmitted, or shared with any third party.

Data We Collect

We collect: parent email address (for account management only), child first name (display only, no surname collected), and quest progress and scanned word history (stored securely on our servers).

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

LexiLore is designed for children ages 5–12 under parental supervision. We do not collect personal information from children under 13 without verified parental consent. A parental consent flow is required before any child profile is created.

Data Deletion

Parents may request deletion of all account data at any time from within the app under Parent Dashboard → Request Data Deletion. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for secure data storage and Sentry for crash reporting (diagnostic data only, not linked to user identity). No data is shared with advertisers.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: beautifulhearts.n@gmail.com
